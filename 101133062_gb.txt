George Brown College is known for providing students with the skills,
hands-on experience and key factors in order to establish a strong 
foundation for their pursuit of success. A wide range of programs are
offered from the 3 campuses located across the core of the city. 
With more than 4,000 international students, the college offers a 
diverse and active learning environment for all kinds of students. 
It also provides practical experience through personal assistance
and advice as well. Last but not least, George Brown College was 
also named as one of Toronto's top employer and best research 
college in 2014.
