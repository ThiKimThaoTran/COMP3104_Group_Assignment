The importance of learning DevOps Engineer in the software development cycle.

    - Helps strengthen the link between development and operations teams. At the same time improve cross-functional working ability.

    - Increase release rate regularly to improve product quickly and release features faster, as well as fix bugs in a timely manner, 
    serve users, be relevant to the market, and easily adjust business performance.

    - Take advantage of automation tools, reduce risk, reduce the failure rate, improve reliability.

    - If there is an error, the product recovery time is faster.

    - Easily manage complex or scalable systems, upgrade the environment more favourable.

    - DevOps helps to migrate without sacrificing security because of configuration management policies, controls, and methods.
